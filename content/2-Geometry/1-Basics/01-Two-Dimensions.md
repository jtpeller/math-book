## Overview

{{< dfn "Two-Dimensional Space" >}} is space with two dimensions.

These two dimensions are commonly called "length" and "width".

You can think of Two-Dimensional Space (or, 2D Space) like a sheet of paper. When you draw on a paper, you have two ways you can go: vertically (up / down) and horizontally (left / right). This gives you the two dimensions to work with.

## Geometric Objects in 2D Space

In 2D space, there are plenty of geometric objects that can exist. As mentioned previously, one-dimensional space can contain points and lines, as can two dimensional space.

Plus, 2D space can also include new objects, like shapes, angles, vectors, and more.
