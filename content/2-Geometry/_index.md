## Overview

{{< dfn "Geometry" >}} is a subject entrenched in the study of shapes, dimensions, space, and more. It began most simply as studying real physical shapes, and has since expanded into intangible topics like four (or higher) dimensions and more.

Geometry is an incredibly important field in Applied Mathematics fields like Physics, as it is often required to model physical situations (for instance, projectile paths). Therefore, Geometry is an incredibly important part of mathematics.
